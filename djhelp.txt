>>> Lolium DJ Commands <<<
Lolium offers several modes for DJ's to get information.
-sendnormal >>> Requests will be sent in PM, changes in listeners will be sent in a notice.
-sendnotice >>> Requests and changes in listeners will be sent in notices.
-sendpm >>> Requests and changes in listeners will be sent in personal messages.
-sendstaff >>> Requests will be sent to the staff room, and changes in listeners in a notice.
>>> END <<<
